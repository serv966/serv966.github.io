USB Block

SCHEMATIC DIAGRAM for USB-BLK

The hub controlled complies with the 1 up stream and 4 down stream. Up to 4 USB corresponded peripherals can be connected. It judges the communication speed (full-speed or low-speed) automatically and corresponds both. The power supply is 5V. The U852 supplies the operation clock (6MHz) to 19pin. The monitor controller (U852) is used for the monitor adjustment. It operates at 3.3V of the power supply and at 12MHz of the operation clock. It is controlled through the I2C bus by the microprocessor.
The CN855 is the up stream connector. The differential data signal with the host are input to 1 pin and power supply from the host (5V) is input to the 1 pin. The microprocessor (U351) monitors the voltage (USB_DET) and detects the connection to the up stream.
The data signal from the host is input to 28 and 29 pin of U851.
The CN852~CN854 and CN856 are down stream connector. The power (5V_U_OUT) is supplied to down stream from the 1 pin of the each connectors. As well as the up stream, 2 and 3 pin is the data signal and connected to U851.
When the over-current control IC (U853) detects the over-current, it makes each connector stop supplying the power to each downstream.
