High Voltage Output Circuit
This circuit generates resonance voltage pulse by the switching of the high voltage FET (Q701) at the primary line of FBT (T701). It is boosted by the FBT and generates the high voltage. In order to stabilize the high voltage causing load changing, the high voltage is bled at the resistance and input to the high voltage control IC U701 (4 pin). This IC controls and stabilizes the high voltage by controlling the switching pulse width of the high voltage FET (Q701) . This switching pulse is output from the control IC 1 pin.
If horizontal output circuit stops by some trouble, the circuit stops high voltage circuit by preventing input of the 2fH to the control IC.
The same conventional X-ray detection method is still used. However, when the detection voltage (MON-XP) is input to the micro-controller, and the preset reference voltage is exceeded, the micro-controller causes entry to the power save mode, and the protector display is indicated on the LEDs. The reference voltage differs according to each individual Set. The detection voltage is read by the micro-controller under predetermined conditions. The voltage obtained by multiplying a fixed ratio becomes the reference voltage, and this is stored to memory. When some trouble such as layer shorting of the FBT secondary wiring (HV-8pin) causes the FBT tertiary wiring (5-7pin) output to drop, this drop is detected by Q724 to switch the MON-XP detection voltage to the micro-controller to High, and shift to protector operation in the same way.
The voltage obtained by rectifying that waveform output from the 4pin and 1 pin of the FBT is used as the power supply for the cut off control circuit in the video circuit and VDF circuit.
DF Circuit
The DF wave IC (U761) generates horizontal and vertical parabola wave for the focus. (H.DF, V.DF). The H. DRV and V.SAW is the sync signal for each wave and input to the 17 and 2 pin.
The U761 amplifies the H.DF/V.DF and controls the phase of H.DF. It is controlled by the output voltage from the D/A converter (U570).
The H.DF is amplified and boosted at the Q760~Q762. The V.DF is amplified at Q763 and Q764 and impressed to the focus terminal (13 pin) of the FBT.
