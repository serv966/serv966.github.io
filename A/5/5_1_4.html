Video Block

SCHEMATIC DIAGRAM for VIDEO BLK

The signal coming from the D-sub and/or BNC is input to the Analog switch IC (U201). The selected video signal (D-SUB or BNC) is output to the video-Pre-Amplifier (U202) from U201. Then, the GAIN of R, G and B signals from the U201 are controlled by U202 and these signals are mixed with the OSD signal . The R, G and B signals are amplified by the video output amplifier (U203). Then the cutoff level control IC (U204) controls the cutoff level of R, G and B signals output from the U203, and controls the black level of the R, G and B signals from U203.

Input Select (U201)
The U201 builds in the input signal switching circuit, the sync separator circuit.
The selected input signal by U201 is displayed on screen. The selected control signal (D/B) is input to the 18pin through the measure gate array (U351).
The sync separator circuit built in outputs G on Sync signal at D-Sub input to the measure gate array as the composite signal at TTL level.
Video Pre Amplifier (U202)
The U202 controls the R, G and B gain signals coming from the input select IC (U201), and mixes the video signal and the OSD signal. The microprocessor (U351) controls these operations of the U202 through the I2C bus. And the U202 has the following built-in functions; the Sync Separator Circuit, the video blanking, clamp, D/A converter functions, Video signal detection circuit, Sharpness and Gamma functions. The G, R and B signals coming from the U205 are input to the OSD G, OSD R, OSD B of the U202. And the BLK of the U205 is input to the 1pin of OSD BLK to make the display period of the video signal blank.
The signal controlled in Gain is output from R OUT, G OUT and B OUT.
The Sync Separator circuit outputs the G on Sync signal at BNC input to the measure gate array as the composite signal at TTL level. The clamp pulse is input to 21pin (CP) and the video blanking pulse is input to 30pin (BLK). The D/A converter is used for the ABL and Signal Filter adjustment.
As the Auto Sizing function, this IC detects the video signal of each color to output the display period of the video signal to the measure gate array from 17 pin (DISP).
OSD (U205)
The IC is controlled by microprocessor (U351) through the three lined serial bus. The IC uses the OSD_H and OSD_V output from measure gate array as the sync signal. The displayed OSD signal which is output from R, G, B is mixed with the video signal by the U202.
Video Output Amplifier (U203)
The video signal output from the U202 is input to 2, 8, and 12pin. The video signals are amplified and each signal is output from 4, 6, 14 pin. This amplified signal drives the CRT cathode. The Signal Filter 1/2 function can reduce video card's smear or ghost by switching emitter peaking constant of U203.
Cut Off Control (U204, U208)
The cut off level of video signals is controlled in the cut off circuit (U204). The cut off level is controlled by changing by the DC level of the video signals that are output from U203 in the U204 output of IROUT, AIGOUT, AIBOUT. The amount of change is controlled by PWM output of measure-gate allay.
DDC (U101)
The U101 has the information for product ID, serial No or corresponded frequency. The information is read when the minter is connected to the DDC computer. This IC corresponds to the DDC2B. SDA connects with the 12pin of D-Sub connector, SCL with 15 pin.
The power supply of the U101 is generated from the input sync signal and 5V_S.
