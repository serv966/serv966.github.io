Horizontal Drive Circuit
This circuit amplifies the pulse voltage of the H.DRV the horizontal oscillate circuit (U602) generated and forms the shape of the wave.
The horizontal drive circuit consists of the Drive FET (Q502) and the Drive transformer (T502). The drive current is supplied by the constant-current circuit (Q505 and Q506). The drive electric current is controlled in the DAC (U551 9pin) voltage.
Horizontal Output circuit
The Output transistor Q501 works as the switching function. The Horizontal output transistor Q501 outputs a saw-tooth current wave to the Deflection Yolk coil by using the Damper Diode (D501) and the Resonance Capacitors (C501, C502). The collector pulse of the Q501 is output as the H. FBP to the secondary winding side of the H.OUT transformer T501. This pulse drives the DC-DC converter circuit and is also used as the video blanking pulse.
Horizontal Linearity Correction circuit (PCB-LIN)
This circuit stands on the PCB-MAIN. The Linearity coils (L570, L571) and the Choke coil (L572, L573) are the linearity correction coils. And the capacitors (C571~C580) are the linearity correction capacitor. The Q571~Q578 are FET. The switching of the Cs condenser is done with FET (Q570, Q571~Q573), and a switching control signal is outputted from DAC (U570).
Distortion Correction Output Circuit
The parabola wave (S.PIN.) generated in the DSP Block, is amplified in the U571. The amplified wave in the U571 are superimposed on the H.+B voltage by the output transistor Q578 and thus distortion is corrected.
H.Size Control Circuit
The horizontal size control circuit fixes the FBP from T501 on the secondary winding side. That voltage is controlled by the voltage from the D/A converter (U551 11pin, 12pin).
The controlled voltage is output to the Power Block as ERR (Error). In the Power Block, H.+B voltage is controlled by the ERR voltage level, and as a result, the Horizontal size is changed.
Raster Shift Control Circuit (PCB-LIN)
The five-step switching of the SW570 and the SW571 changes the inflow current to H.+B to move the raster. The SW570 controls the right and left movement. The SW571 controls the variable volume.
Vertical Deflection Block Circuit
The output IC (U401) amplifies the V.DRV which is generated in U601 and supplies the deflection current to the Vertical Deflection York. This wave is generated by digital, the V.DRV is staircase-saw-tooth waveform. If you drive this vertical output with this wave, the horizontal noise appears on the screen. So, the filter circuit (Q401~Q404) smoothes the wave and drive the vertical output circuit.
At the filter circuit, the V.BLK2 pulse prevents the wave bluntness during the vertical blanking porch.
The V.P.DET is used at AutoSize function and protector function of the microprocessor at the control block. The rectangle wave is generated from the vertical deflection wave through the comparator.(U402)
Because insulating is not done between U401 and AC401 radiator, the radiator is always impressed with VEE voltage (about 14V). Please be minded not to connect the radiator and chassisGND when you deconstruct it.
Landing Correction output circuit
The U451 and U452 are power-ope amp to drive the landing correction coil of 4 corners. Each correction are controlled by the voltage output from the D/A converter (U551).
Convergence, Tilt and LCC circuit
The U453 are power-ope amp to drive the V.Convergence Circuit and LCC. The U454 are one to drive the H.Convergence Circuit and Tilt. Tilt and H/V. Convergence correction are controlled by the voltage output from the D/A converter (U552). The LCC correction is controlled by that from U551. The V.Convergence circuit mixes and outputs the parabola wave of top-bottom division generated in the DSP block.
