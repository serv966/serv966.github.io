Caution on Anode Cap

When putting the CRT anode lead back onto th CRT, extra care should be taken as follows.

Mistake in handling may result in damage or electric shock.

  1. Anode button area should be neat and clean. If the area is dirty, blow off all dust and dirt after the work is done.
  2. Silicon Grease (Shinetsu KS-650N) should be applied equally around the area using a paint-brush.
  3. Subject area to be applied with Grease is from 10mm from Anode button through 5mm beyond the Anode lead cap brim.
  4. Anode lead hook should be securely caught inside Anode button.
  5. X-ray protection metal inside Anode lead cap should be securely stuck, covering the CRT's Anode button.
  6. Anode lead cap shall be completely stuck on the surface and any partial tearing or lifting of it should not be recognized.
  7. Make sure that there is space (20~30mm) between the Anode lead cap and the DG coil & Rotation Coil.
