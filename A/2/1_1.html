Precautions

Read and completely understand the Primary Service Manual before attempting service.


Unplug the power cord before servicing.

The conductive area may become high voltage which can result in an electric shock or other hazardous danger.


Do not touch the sharp edge of the chassis.

It can result in injury.

Before starting to disassemble and assemble, the following tools are necessary.

  • Plus driver (No.2)
  • Minus driver
  • 6 angle box driver x 8mm
  • Nipper
  • Pliers
  • Solder iron
  • Solder
