Shield Removal

  1. Remove the BACK COVER.
  2. Remove 6 screws (c) from the top of the monitor.
  3. Remove the TOP-SHIELD from the chassis.
  4. Remove 4 screws (d) from the top and rear of the monitor.
  5. Remove the Anode Lead from the WIRE-HOLDER to remove each SIDE-SHIELD.
  6. Remove 2 screws (e) from the rear of the monitor to remove the REAR-SHIELD.
