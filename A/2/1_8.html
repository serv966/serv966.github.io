ASSY CRT Removal

Take care not to scratch the coating of the Degauss Coil.

This may cause electric shock or damage. Read and completely understand "Caution on Degauss Coil" before servicing.
-

Discharge the high voltage completely before removing Anode Cap.

Unplug the power cord before discharging. When removing Anode Cap from the CRT, read the primary service manual and make sure that the high voltage is completely discharged. This may cause an electric shock.


Do not lift the CRT by the neck.

When removing the CRT, hold not just the neck but the whole body with both hands. The CRT neck is easy to break structurally. Dropping or breaking it may result in injury.

  1. Take steps (to Front Panel Removal). 
  2. Remove the Anode Cap from the CRT.
  3. Remove tge PCB-CRT from the CRT neck.
  4. Cut a clamper.
  5. Pull out 2 connectors (CN501, CN453) from the PCB-MAIN
  6. Remove 4 nuts (r) from the CRT and remove the CRT.

 Each corner is same

When equipping CRT, do not let a leads to PCB-SW caught in the FRAME. And, fix with nuts with the CRT surface tilted down.
