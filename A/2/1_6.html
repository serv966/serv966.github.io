FRONT PANEL Removal

  1. Remove the SHIELD.
  2. Remove 4 screws (m) holding the Front Panel from the rear.
  3. Hold down the stopper of the USB downstream port cover on the front panel and remove a screw (n) holding the ASSY CN UNIT and the connector (CN856) from the VIDEO-UNIT and remove the ASSY CN UNIT.
  4. Slide the Front Panel slightly from the monitor and pull out a connector (CN841) from the PCB-SW and take off the Front Panel.
