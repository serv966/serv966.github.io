ASSY VIDEO UNIT Removal

  1. Remove the REAR SHIELD.
  2. Pull out 2 connectors (CNH30, CNH31) from the PCB-CRT and a connector (CN856) from the PCB-VIDEO.
  3. Remove 4 screws (f) from the side and rear of the monitor.
  4. Lift and remove the VIDEO-UNIT from the rear of the monitor.

(PCB-VIDEO Removal)

  1. Remove the ASS'Y VIDEO UNIT taking the above steps.
  2. Remove 7 screws (g) from the VIDEO UNIT to take off the shield covering the parrts side of the PCB-VIDEO.
  3. Remove the shield covering the solder side with the soldering iron to remove the PCB.

When fixing the PCB-VIDEO, tighten firstly with the marked screw (g).
