PCB-SW Removal

  1. Remove the FRONT-PANEL.
  2. Remove 2 pretection covers for the speaker terminal (COVER-B) on the bottom of the Front Panel with the minus screw driver from the Front Panel.
  3. Remove 2 speaker terminals and 2 screws (q) from the PCB-SW.
  4. Unfold the stopper of the Front Panel holding STICK-SW and remove the PCB.
