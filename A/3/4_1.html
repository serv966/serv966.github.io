Setting the Monitor

The image of CRT display screen may be altered by the earth magnetism field depending on which direction it is facing. For correct adjustment, check the East to West magnetic axis at your work bench and align the neck of the CRT to it.

Before the Adjustment

Please perform the following things before starting the ITC Adjustment.

*Manual Degaussing (If it can not be helped)
Using a Degaussing Bar, thoroughly degauss CRT surface and around the display.

When using the Degaussing Bar, please follow the instructions below. Otherwise, loss of the picture quality or damage to the monitor may result.
When degaussing, perform the "Reset" of ScreenManager "Uniformity".

  1. Do not turn Degaussing Bar on and off closely next to the monitor.
  2. Use the Degaussing Bar more than 5cm away from the monitor.
  3. When degaussing the CRT surface, start from the front of the CRT and slowly get it away by circling the Degaussing Bar. Turn OFF the power switch more than 1.5m away from the monitor.
