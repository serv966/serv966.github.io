Adjustments by Magnets

After performing the adjustments shown previously in this section, use RED and GREEN Magnets, Ferrite Sheets, or Spoiler Magnets on the CRT funnel to adjust more delicate misconvergence.

In ITC adjustment, a adjustment for one part will affect other parts.
For example, convergence adjustment with magnet may cause wider distortion.
Please try to get comprehensive adjustment as a whole, as perfect adjustment for all fields is impossible.
